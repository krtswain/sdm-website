Multiple Clubs - One Tradition - For all Enthusiasts

SDMCET has a strong tradition of student bodies and clubs that attract students from all disciplines. It is one of the features that infuses Dharwad with a singular charm, a unique advantage over other university campuses.

Robotics & Aero modelling clubs

The Robotics Club and the Aero Modelling Club are an active example, motivating students from various disciplines. The club distributes low cost kits, conducts workshops and also organizes competitions. Annual workshops, under guidance from experts, are organized regularly to encourage more students to join the club. Competitions are held after the workshop and prizes are awarded to encourage students.

Literary Clubs

The Reflection Team of SDMCET strives to hone the all-round talents of an individual who wishes to master the universal language – ENGLISH. It facilitates a variety of events that engage you as a listener and a participant. The club hosts literary events like what’s the good work, mock press, dumb charades , debates , ship wreck , sansad sangram(mock parliament ) and a lot more events which serve as great platforms for the students of SDMCET to exhibit their written and speaking talents. It is responsible for nearly 15 activities in Insignia every year. Team Reflections possesses the seriousness associated with a literature club however it also possesses the fun, energy and enthusiasm associated with any college club.

QWERTY.I/O

QWERTY.I/O is an SDMCET student body to provide its students with the complete skillset that an engineering student needs to survive and flourish in the corporate world. Established in 2018, QWERTY has a history of conducting multiple workshops every semester and providing quality events. With a current strength of 30 members and growing, the club holds auditions every year for new members through its events.

Pratibimb

Pratibimb is a Dramatics club of SDMCET that was established in 2009 and specialising in drama, specifically Street play, Mad ads, and Skit in Hindi. With a current strength of 51 and 47 alumni passouts, Pratibimb recruits members every year through both fresher's as well as auditions.
